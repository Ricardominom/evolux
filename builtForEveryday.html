Why LVP floors?

Durability

Resistant to scratches, dents, stains. Great for busyhouseholds with kids and pets

Water resistant

Ideal for use in areas prone to moisture such as kitchens, bathrooms and basements

Comfort

LVP floors are warmer and softer to walk on compared to other floors

Realism

Modern printing technology allows LVP to mimic natural looks of traditional floors like wood and tile

Easy installation

Click products are faster to install and require less tools. Perfect for DIY. Repairs are simple and quick!

Ease of maintenance

Sweep, Vacuum, and occasional damp mop with mild neutral Ph cleaners. (No abrasive cleaners)

Sound absorption

Clicks with pad attached help reduce noise levels
